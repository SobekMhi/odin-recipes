Meat ball

Description

Turkey, beef, and Italian sausage meatballs that go perfectly with your favorite marinara sauce. Serve over pasta!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 eggs
  • Â½ cup quick cooking oats
  • 1 cup vegetable oil, or as needed

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Combine the Ingredients in a large bowl. Shape into 2-inch diameter balls.
  3. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes.
